About Naturz Industries

Naturz Industries Sdn Bhd [202001019018 (1375338-K)]  founded in 2020, is a leading exporter of palm oil and its derivatives.

Our business encompasses the manufacturing, marketing, and exporting of palm oil, palm olein, shortening, vegetable ghee, speciality fats, palm stearin, and frying fat.

With extensive industry know-how, we maximize value addition on our Malaysia made products for our valued customers worldwide, enabling them to create brand equity while maximizing export revenue and profit.

With the goal of becoming one of the leading palm oil exporting company in Malaysia within the next three years, we work relentlessly to achieve our goals by collaborating with and leveraging our team members, bankers, suppliers, and buyers.

Backed by a strong commitment to creating immense value for our customers, our expert team strives to incorporate trust, reliability, and transparency in everything we do.
