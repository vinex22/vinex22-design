## Typography

- Display: Newsreader, Georgia, serif
- Body: Manrope, system sans-serif
- Data / labels: JetBrains Mono

## Exhibition design requirements

- Large claims only: booth viewers have 2-5 seconds per slide.
- Product visuals must be primary, not decorative.
- Use official facts only. Avoid unverifiable export volumes, country counts, certifications, or customer claims unless supplied by Naturz.
- CTA should show website, email and phone from the official site.
