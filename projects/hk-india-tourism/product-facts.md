# Hong Kong India Tourism Campaign · Facts & Sources
> Captured on: 2026-05-03
> Purpose: factual guardrails for a 15-slide campaign deck aimed at Indian travellers.

## Verified facts used in the deck

- Hong Kong Tourism Board's official India-facing site lists must-see attractions including Hong Kong Disneyland, Ocean Park Hong Kong, Avenue of Stars, The Big Buddha, and The Peak.
  Source: https://www.discoverhongkong.com/in/index.html

- Hong Kong Tourism Board's attractions page lists Victoria Harbour, Hong Kong Disneyland, Ocean Park Hong Kong, Water World Ocean Park, The Peak, Kai Tak Sports Park, West Kowloon Cultural District, and A Symphony of Lights among recommended destinations.
  Source: https://www.discoverhongkong.com/in/explore/attractions.html

- Indian nationals have an official Hong Kong Immigration Department pre-arrival registration page. The deck says "check pre-arrival registration before booking" rather than giving legal advice.
  Source: https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/pre-arrival_registration_for_indian_nationals.html

- HKTB's Big Buddha page says the Tian Tan Buddha is 34 metres including its base, reached by 268 steps, and completed in 1993.
  Source: https://www.discoverhongkong.com/eng/place-to-go/travel.guide-the-big-buddha.html

## Honest placeholders / caveats

- Package pricing is intentionally left as `QUOTE LIVE` because airfares, hotel rates, attraction tickets, and exchange rates change constantly.
- Opening hours, crowding, transport disruptions, and immigration requirements should be checked before travel.
- The deck is a campaign concept, not a legal travel advisory or final tour operator quotation.
